What we do

Three focused offerings for international schools and educators in India.

Staff Hiring for International Schools

Full‑cycle recruitment for IB and Cambridge schools in India—leadership, coordinators, subject teachers, and support staff. Rigorous vetting aligned to international standards.

  • Role definition and scorecards
  • Sourcing, screening, and demo class reviews
  • Background checks and reference verification
  • Offer management and onboarding support

Authorization & Accreditation Support

Hands‑on guidance to achieve and maintain recognition from IB, Cambridge, CIS, NEASC, and WASC—documentation, readiness audits, and visit preparation.

  • Gap analysis against frameworks
  • Policy and handbook development
  • Evidence gathering and action planning
  • Mock visits and team coaching

School Improvement & Development Plans

End‑to‑end planning from concept to launch: approvals, NOC, infrastructure readiness, and continuous improvement roadmaps tailored to international benchmarks.

  • Project planning and regulatory clearances
  • Curriculum and staffing models
  • Quality assurance and PD frameworks
  • Operational SOPs and governance

How we work

Transparent, collaborative, and outcome‑driven engagements.

  1. Discovery

    Needs assessment and scope definition with all stakeholders.

  2. Blueprint

    Roadmap, milestones, and quality metrics aligned to IB/Cambridge.

  3. Implementation

    Recruitment, documentation, training, and operational setup.

  4. Review & Readiness

    Internal audits, mock visits, and final readiness checks.
